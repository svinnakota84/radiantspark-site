About Radiant Spark Global

Radiant Sparkle Distribution Industries Pvt Ltd is a premier multi-sector enterprise delivering excellence and innovation across the rapidly developing landscapes of Guntur and Amaravati, Andhra Pradesh — with a specialised technology arm, Radiant Spark LLC, operating in the United States.

We are a unified corporate entity, synergising key industries — renewables, F&B, distribution, logistics, and IT — under one professional global banner.

Our Integrated Divisions

  • Renewables: Solar & EV Infrastructure
    Developing solar power solutions and a robust EV charging station network to build a sustainable future.
  • Sparkle Beverages
    Home of the Sparkle Brand Drinks — wholesale and logistics with full FSSAI licensing and GST compliance.
  • Sparkle Distribution Network
    Our own in-house distribution and last-mile delivery fleet, eliminating third-party dependencies and powered by solar energy.
  • Information Technology & Consulting (USA)
    Radiant Spark LLC delivers Business Transformation, Digital Transformation, Artificial Intelligence, Technology, Solutions, IT Consulting and IT Staffing services across the United States.

Creating a Unified Synergy Hub
