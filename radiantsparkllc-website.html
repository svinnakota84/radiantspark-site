About Radiant Sparkle

Radiant Sparkle Distribution Industries Pvt Ltd is a premier multi-sector enterprise dedicated to delivering excellence and innovation across the rapidly developing landscapes of Guntur and Amaravati, Andhra Pradesh. We are a unified corporate entity, synergizing key industries—renewables, F&B distribution, logistics, and IT—under one professional banner.

Our unique integrated model leverages our diversified portfolio to create a truly seamless and efficient hub for distribution, logistics, and innovation.

Our Integrated Divisions

  • Renewables: Solar & EV Infrastructure
    Developing Solar Power Solutions and a robust EV Charging Station Network to build a sustainable future.
  • Food & Beverages Distribution
    Home of the Sparkle Brand Drinks. We provide wholesale and logistics with full FSSAI licensing and GST compliance.
  • Logistics Partner: Xpressbees Delivery
    A professional Cargo Business and Franchise Partner for Xpressbees, providing reliable first- and last-mile delivery.
  • Information Technology & Consulting
    A specialized wing providing SQL troubleshooting, Fusion HCM Consulting, and advanced technical infrastructure.

Creating a Unified Synergy Hub

We operate as a strategically synchronized system. Our delivery vans serve multiple roles, distributing F&B and Xpressbees cargo from a central hub. In our commitment to sustainability, we strive to power our entire operations using our own clean, solar energy.
